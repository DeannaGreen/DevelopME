What is meditation?

Meditation is a set of techniques that are intended to encourage a heightened state of awareness and focused attention.

Why should I meditate?

Practising meditation on a regular basis as a developer will help you to train your mind to live with more awareness. This awareness and focus will help you to write better code and efficient programmes.

How long should I meditate for?

It is completely up to you. If you are new to meditation we would recommend starting of with 2 minutes and then work your way up.
